Instructions:

Use the left click to uncover a spot(beware of the mines!) and the right click to mark spots you suspect to be mines.

once all mines have been marked and all empty spots uncovered, you win!

Difficulties:

    There are 4 difficulties:
  • Easy: 4x4 Board with 2 mines
  • Normal: 8x8 Board with 12 mines
  • Hard: 12x12 Board with 30 mines
  • Manual: 12x12 Board and you place mines manually, click it again to start the game

Hints:

You can use a hint 3 times by clicking on one of the lighbulbs

After you use one, your next click will reveal the spot you chose and all those around it, without triggering any bombs!

They will be hidden again after a second, so pay attention!

Safe Click

Will mark a spot that does not have a mine for a couple of seconds.
